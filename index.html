Sihong He

Hi, my name is Sihong He. I am a 3rd-year Ph.D. student in computer science and engineering at the University of Connecticut working with Prof. Fei Miao.

My research interests include

  • Reinforcement Learning (RL, MARL, Robust RL, Robust MARL)
  • Optimization (Robust Optimization, Distributionally Robust Optimization)
  • Smart City (AMoD System, Electric Vehicle)
  • Diversified Portfolios

[Open to Collaboration]  I am looking for undergraduate and master's students to work on: (1) robust reinforcement learning, (2) multi-agent reinforcement learning, and (3) smart city. If you are interested in these topics and strongly self-motivated, feel free to send me an email with your resume and the interesting topic. UConn and non-UConn Students are both welcome, and students who would like to apply to Ph.D. programs are extremely welcome.
